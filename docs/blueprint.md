# **App Name**: Nexa International Reimagined

## Core Features:

- Account Overview Dashboard: Provides a consolidated view of all accounts, balances, and recent transactions. Includes visual graphs for tracking spending and income.
- Funds Transfer: Enables users to transfer funds between their accounts and to external accounts, with options for setting up recurring transfers.
- Bill Payment: Allows users to schedule and pay bills directly from the app, with support for adding and managing payees.
- Transaction History: Displays a detailed history of all transactions, with filtering and search options.
- Card Management: Allows users to manage their debit and credit cards, including viewing card details, reporting lost or stolen cards, and activating new cards.
- Customer Support Chatbot: AI-powered chatbot for instant customer support, capable of answering common questions, guiding users through tasks, and escalating complex issues to human support agents as a tool.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1A237E) to convey trust and stability.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#E8EAF6), providing a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Vibrant orange (#FF5722) to highlight important actions and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a versatile sans-serif, for clear and accessible text.
- Use simple, line-based icons to represent various banking features and actions.
- Employ a clean, grid-based layout for a consistent and organized user interface.
- Incorporate subtle animations for transitions and feedback to enhance user engagement.
